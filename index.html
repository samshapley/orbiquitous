orbiquitous is the vision of a platform for AI augmented universal exploration.

Space information is disjointed, and spread across the web in varying formats and accessibility. The aim is to create a unified platform that provides a seamless experience for users to explore space data, learn about space technology, and interact with AI agents to get answers to their questions.

There is a vast wealth of publicly available data from NASA, ESA, and all global space agencies that can be used to create a rich and gamified experience for users. Some future features include:

  • Fully immersive replicas of planets, showing real-time data of satellites, rovers and weather patterns
  • Patent explorer to understand the latest space technology, and how you can build on it
  • Exoplanet simulators to explore the universe beyond our solar system, and understand the science behind detection and classification
  • General space news and updates
  • Agentic tutor for aerospace engineering, astronomy, cosmology and all things space

Space should be accessible. AI is an accessibility accelerant. Let's make space knowledge ubiquitous.
